Smoothie

Description

A refreshing and tropical smoothie blending sweet strawberries, creamy bananas, and tangy pineapple for a perfect balance of flavors. Packed with vitamins and natural sweetness, it's a deliciously healthy treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup yogurt
  • ¼ cup pineapple juice
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt, pineapple juice, sugar, orange juice, and milk in a blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
